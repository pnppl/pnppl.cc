    Vegan Perfumeries ¬ §

    #vegan #perfume

    It's basically impossible to verify vendors' claims about their ingredients, other than by checking if they contradict themselves. Some advertise themselves as vegan but then make exceptions. Most perfume ingredients are plant-based now, but boutique perfumers think it's cute and romantic to use animals. Indie sellers are certainly not doing any animal testing themselves, though their ingredient suppliers might be.
